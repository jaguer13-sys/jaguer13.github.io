    • Devin Booker

    Devin Booker is an American professional basketball player for the Phoenix Suns of the National Basketball Association (NBA). He played college basketball for the Kentucky Wildcats. Booker was selected by the Suns with the 13th overall pick in the 2015 NBA draft.

    Career Highlights

    Devin Booker has achieved numerous accolades throughout his career, including multiple All-Star selections and setting franchise records for the Phoenix Suns. He is known for his scoring ability, particularly his proficiency in three-point shooting and clutch performances in critical games.
